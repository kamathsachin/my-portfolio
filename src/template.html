About me

I am on a mission to help organizations successfully optimize their business processes and build technology solutions. I do this by bringing forth my 15+ years of experience in IT Software Development and Application Design. I believe that successful product management is a cross-section between business strategy, consumer insights, team efficiency and a good execution plan.

My areas of expertise include managing multi-million-dollar digital transformation projects, leading large scale initiatives with geographically distributed and cross-functional teams, maintaining the product backlog and its prioritization based on organization's vision, project development methodologies like Agile, Scrum and Waterfall and P&C Insurance.
